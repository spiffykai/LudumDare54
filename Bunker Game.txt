Bunker Game
Limited space for defenses and resource generation
Every round you can change layout and remove or add objects
Can go rounds without resource generation, but will lose resources over time
Can go rounds without defense, but if there is not enough defense that round your bunker takes damage
If your bunker is fully damaged or out of a resource, you lose
Might slowly get more people in your bunker to make you lose eventaully
